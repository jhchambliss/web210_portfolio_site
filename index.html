Android Development

"Who Would Win" pits two contestants against each other. Animal, vegetable, or mineral. Superheros, co-workers, living room furniture. The user assigns three variables upon which the contestants will be judged and can determine if any of these variables are more valuable than others. And let the best taco win!

The desire to be able to build this product is what put me on the path back to school. From a nascent idea in the fall 0f 2019, I went from no programming or design experience to deciding to detour a graduate school plan in January of 2020 in order to learn how to build this program.

I submitted this design as my final project for my Android Mobile Development certificate in December that same year.
